About me

My name is nathan Franklin, I am 30 years old and was born in Australia.
For the past 15 years I have been in the hospitality industry managing pubs clubs and restaurants around QLD and the NT.
My prior education includes a year 12 graduate and a cert III in hospitality, my hobbies include online gaming and going to the gym.

My interest in IT

What drew me to IT initially was gaming related, ever since I was a child I have been on computers, consoles, basically anything with a screen.
As I am getting older, I find myself drawn to how these things work rather than just using them.
though I have had no experience in the IT industry it is something I would like to do

Why RMIT?

I decided to study with RMIT for a couple of reasons.
Coming from a smaller town in QLD the local university does not offer IT courses so I had to source elsewhere,
I found that RMIT gave me the best chance to learn as much as I could in a flexible way as I often work night shifts in hospitality.
This does not give me a lot of time during the day as I and up most the night so “conventional” university studies would not suit my schedule very well,
for this I’m am glad to have found RMIT.

What i hope to gain

I am hoping to learn as much as I can during my studies,
there Isn't anything I can think of that will not help me in the future.
Though I would really like to dive into the making of software and everything that comes with it.
I hope to, in the future I hope to move on to game development because that is where my passion lies.
I know this will be a long journey as I know little about the subject, but I am learning more every day.
