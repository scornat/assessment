My project

I would like to create a side scrolling platform game that will be designed based on classics like “Mario Bros” created through the unity platform and marketed through the app store for free. I believe creating a game like this will prove as a great steppingstone for my ideal job by giving me necessary experience in the unity platform and help to understand and learn the steps and skills required to develop a game and work within a development team.

Platform games are very flexible as to the devices they can be played on (e.g. mobile, platform, pc) which makes them incredibly accessible. They are great “entry level” games for anyone who may want to pick up and play them as the rules of the game are simple and easy to understand. The control scheme is also easy to understand and simple to use, Allowing any skill level from seasoned gamer to complete beginner to pick up and play on any device they are comfortable with.

The aim of this game will be simple, the player needs to make it to the end of the stage travelling left to right while performing tasks, collecting items and killing/destroying/avoiding enemies. Deaths will return the player to a checkpoint, which is either the beginning of a stage, A specific point that was previously reached or a whenever a specific task has been completed. The player will have a limited amount of “lives” or “retries” depending on what previous milestones have been reached or tasks completed. Upon running out of “lives” or “retries” the player will need to start from the beginning of the highest stage previously reached. All checkpoints throughout the stage will be reset and will need to be achieved again to be used.

I believe using a color pallet and art style that is bright, bubbly and fun will allow this game to reach more of the market as using things like blood, gore, and horror themes will deter some age groups (especially children) from picking it up and playing it. This will make sure that there is no age constraints of potential players when wanting to play this game and will be safe for gamers of all ages.

Upon opening the game for the first time the player will have to start from stage 1. After the player has completed a stage they can choose to go back and retry the stage completed or continue to a new stage (usually more difficult).

There will be multiple ways a player can lose a “life or a “retry”. Touching an enemy or any projectiles an enemy may shoot will result in a death which will return the player to a checkpoint. Falling off the base platform will also result in a death returning the player to a checkpoint. Running out of time on a stage will also result in a death returning the player to a checkpoint.

Upon completing a stage, A “victory screen” will be shown highlighting any milestones reached, items collected, the total time it took the player to complete the stage and also how many “retries” were used on the stage that was completed.

The making of this game will not require much hardware to do so as much of the work will be done through the unity engine itself. Needing only a laptop with the unity engine installed and somewhere to market the game (a place for potential players to download the game). This game can be made with a small team of people who are familiar with the unity engine and platform games in general and who also have a love for the gaming industry.

If this project were successful I believe it would be not only great experience for game development in general it would also have the opportunity to be marketed as a game that could be sought after for advertisement if it was deemed worthy of being monetized so in future job opportunities I could call on what I learned making this game to give myself a better chance in gaining employment with a game development company.
